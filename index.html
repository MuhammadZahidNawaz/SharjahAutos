About Us

| ہمارے بارے میں

15+ Years of Trust & Quality

پندرہ سالوں سے آپ کی خدمت میں پیش پیش

Welcome to Sharjah Autos & Motors. Located conveniently on Zorkot Road near Rehman Petrol Pump, we have been serving the Sadiqabad community for over a decade and a half.

Under the expert ownership of Muhammad Zahid Nawaz, we specialize in the bikes you ride every day. From the Yamaha YBR 125 to Suzuki, Super Star, and 70cc/Dhoom bikes, we ensure your ride is smooth and reliable.
